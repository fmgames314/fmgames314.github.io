Party Box


So this is the Party Box, or the Rave Box, or the Concert box, or simply a "Boom box". Despite there not being a real name for it, it stil exists. It is composed of a huge storage container with a car system in it. It has a deticated low powered subwoofer and 4 car speakers. 2 of which are 3 way. It has a pioneer head unit as well. Included a built in phone charger, a LED controller, bluetooth and a AUX. It has a built in SLA battery and is also capable of runing off of AC power.
