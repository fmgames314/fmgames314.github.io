Use to be call Cubert but change it to ZOID since this robot is shaped like a trapezoid. So far it has a 2 joint arm and 2 wheelchair motors. Battery and supplemental AC power standard. Will have a gripper on the arm soon too. Also has a 2 way water pump system to import and export water. perhaps automatically watering plants?
